## <a name="create-purchase-order-from-a-work-order"></a><span data-ttu-id="17806-127">สร้างใบสั่งซื้อจากใบสั่งงานป</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="17806-127">Create purchase order from a work order</span></span>
